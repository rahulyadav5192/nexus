About The Group

A global enterprise built across the entire gold value chain.

— 2021 / Established in Sharjah

Nexus began in 2021 in Sharjah as a Gold Bullion and Booking firm. It has since evolved into a diversified, vertically integrated group — sourcing directly at the mines in Africa and moving through refining, manufacturing, wholesale, retail and global investment activities.

Our mission: to offer ethically sourced, high-quality jewelry with premium service — building a balanced ecosystem that pairs traditional asset strength with modern global investment opportunity, and to be recognised for innovation, integrity and operational excellence.
